Math Solver

Math Solver solves numerous mathematical problems with the power of Java. It uses a simple-to-understand layout with a combobox to choose the mathematical operation.

There are a number of mathematical operations in this program:

  • Root of quadratic equation
  • Point of intersection
  • Slope
  • Area of triangle
  • Midpoint
  • Circumference and area of circle
  • Distance between two points
  • Exponent calculator
  • Algebra
  • Vertex of quadratic equation
  • Sine
  • Cosine
  • Tangent
  • Arcsine
  • Arccosine
  • Arctangent
  • Deriviative
  • Integral
  • Exponential from 4 points
  • Exponential from 3 points
  • Sine law (angle)
  • Sine law (side)
  • Cosine law (angle)
  • Cosine law (side)
